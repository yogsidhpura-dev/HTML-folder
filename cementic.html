facts about social media

Social media platforms are widely used globally, with billions of users actively engaging with content daily. These platforms have evolved from simple communication tools to complex ecosystem influencing various aspects of life, including social interaction, business, and even mental well-being.

SixDegrees, launched in 1997, is often regarded as the first social media site. The evolution of online services progressed from serving as channels for networked communication to becoming interactive platforms for networked social interaction with the advent of Web 2.0
